Tel Var Cycle

A boss timer and Tel Var calculator built for console ESO players. PC has an in-game IC boss timer addon — console doesn't. Open this on your phone, tablet, or laptop next to your TV while you farm.

How to use

  1. Tap your alliance (EP / DC / AD) in the Tel Var panel.
  2. Tap each district held by the enemy alliance to apply the bonus.
  3. Set your stone multiplier and group size to match in-game.
  4. Tap Killed on a district when its boss dies — the 15-minute timer starts.
  5. Use Bank when you stash Tel Var, or Ganked if you die (loses 50%).

Timer states

  • Green / ALIVE — boss is up
  • Gold countdown — respawning
  • Amber pulse — under 1 minute
  • Red pulse — under 15 seconds

Tips

  • Guess — find a dead boss? Estimate how many minutes ago it died.
  • Seen Alive — mark a boss up without claiming the kill.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • 1–6 — Killed (district 1–6)
  • S then 1–6 — Seen Alive
  • G then 1–6 — Guess timer
  • R then 1–6 — Reset timer
  • B — Bank · X — Ganked · U — Undo
